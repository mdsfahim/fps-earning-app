Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

About This App

This application ("DailyIncome Bot") is a Telegram Mini App that allows users to earn rewards by completing simple tasks such as watching advertisements, completing assigned tasks, and referring friends. The app is developed and maintained by MD. AL AMIN.

Data We Collect

  • Telegram User ID — Used to uniquely identify your account.
  • Name & Profile Photo — Retrieved from your Telegram profile for display purposes only.
  • Earnings Data — Your balance, total earnings, task history, and referral count are stored to provide the earning service.
  • Withdrawal Information — Payment method and account number you provide when requesting a withdrawal.
  • Proof Screenshots — When you submit proof for proof-based tasks, the screenshot image is stored temporarily until reviewed by an admin.

Referral System

When you share your referral link and someone joins using it, your Telegram User ID is passed as a referral parameter. We record this to credit your referral bonus. No other personal data from the referred user is shared with you.

How We Protect Your Data

  • All data is stored securely in Firebase Realtime Database with appropriate access rules.
  • We do not share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • Your withdrawal details are only visible to the admin for processing payments.
  • Proof screenshots are accessible only to you and the admin for review purposes.

Third-Party Ad Services

This app integrates with third-party advertising networks (such as Adsgram, Monetag, and others) to display ads. These services may collect their own data according to their respective privacy policies. We do not control the data practices of these third-party services.

Withdrawals & Payments

Withdrawal requests are processed manually by the admin. Payment methods and account numbers you provide are used solely for sending your earned funds. We are not responsible for delays caused by payment providers.

Account Deletion

If you wish to have your data deleted, you can contact the developer via the "Developer Contact" option in the app menu. Upon verified request, your account data including balance, task history, and proofs will be permanently deleted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
